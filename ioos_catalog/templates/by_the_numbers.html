About IOOS by the Numbers

These data were obtained from multiple sources on 9/16/2014. Each source is identified on the left with a link to the original source. The federal partners and regional associations are public information and each association can be found on the left under the federal and regional tabs. At the time of collection, there were five QARTOD manuals in-place for IOOS, which describe a comprehensive method for QA/QC of oceanographpic data. The number of datasets available on the IOOS catalog are available on the dashboard. The number of glider days is as of 2013. More information about gliders may be found on our gliders page. Our services number is available on our dashboard , a service describes an endpoint where clients may obtain either data or metadata about oceanographic datasets. The number of servers was calculated by totaling the number of top-level-domains available for each partner and association for IOOS. The number of platforms is from public information available on the IOOS homepage.
